Brought to you by Team CRY, WordPlay offers a new way to work. Simply specify the amount of time you would like to spend on your essay and when that time elapses, you will be reqarded with a game break. Offering incentive to work will make you more productive by giving your brain small breaks to remain focused while working. So enter a time and get ready to write!
